Kansas Linux Fest

a Yearly Open Source (Free/Libre Open Source Software) and Open Knowledge Festival for Kansas

Welcome to the Kansas Linux Fest

This is a project of the Free/Libre Open Source and Open Knowledge Association of Kansas (www.openkansas.us) and other organizations. We are still working on the board of directors for the conference and planning. We are planning on the date of 21-22 March 2015, we are still looking for a venue and are trying to book either the Lawrence Public Library or to hold it at KU. Otherwise there are more options in Town.
